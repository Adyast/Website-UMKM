Our Story

Where the city sleeps, we brew. Discover the origins of your quiet sanctuary.

Our Roots

Nocturne began as a simple idea in a crowded, noisy world: what if a coffee shop could be more than a caffeine stop? What if it could be a pause button?

Founded by three friends who found their best conversations happened after midnight, we sought to recreate that specific clarity. We traveled from the highlands of Ethiopia to the minimalist cafes of Kyoto, gathering inspiration to build a space that honors the ritual of coffee and the power of silence.

Our Craft

Coffee is a science as much as it is an art. We treat every bean with the reverence it deserves, roasting in small batches to highlight distinct flavor profiles.

  • check_circle Single-origin beans sourced directly from sustainable farms.
  • check_circle Precision brewing methods using V60 and Chemex.
  • check_circle Baristas trained in the intricate art of extraction.

Our Space

The environment you drink coffee in is just as important as the coffee itself. Nocturne is designed with intent—dark tones to soothe the eyes, soft lighting to focus the mind, and acoustics that dampen the outside chaos.

Whether you are writing your next novel, coding late into the night, or simply enjoying a moment of solitude, our space adapts to your rhythm.
